Martzi Campos

Martzi Campos is an artist/interactive designer living in Los Angeles. She has a BFA from Rhode Island School of Design where she studied Painting and Installation art and a MFA in Interactive Media and Games from the University of Southern California. She has designed many interactive works ranging from VR art installation to Pop up books that control video games. Her main focus and passion is on combining handcrafted objects with digital technology to create magical interactions and experiences. She has show her work at E3, IndieCade, DIGRAPH and the Game Developers Conference. She is currently working at the Game Innovation Lab and teaching Game Design at USC.
