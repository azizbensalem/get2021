Wafa Bourkhis

Wafa Bourkhis is a Tunisian multidisciplinary artist (painting, engraving, digital art, video art). She has exhibited her works in many events and cultural spaces such as the Mediterranean Arts Festival, the Festival OFF in Avignon, the Cerveira Biennial and the Douro Engraving Biennial in Portugal and the Zero One Biennial in California. She has collaborated with many international artists like Fred Forest, Mark Skwarek, Patrick Lichty, etc. She obtained her doctorate in 2013 entitled: Digital territories as spaces of artistic creation, to obtain her doctorate in sciences and techniques of the arts at the universities of Tunis and Artois (France), and this under the co-supervision of Pr. She participated in various academic events as a member of the Research Laboratory on Culture, New Technologies and Development at the University of Tunis headed by Dr Mohamed Zinelabidine, the ex-Minister of Cultural Affairs. She has presented several communications at numerous conferences and symposia in Italy, the United Kingdom, France and Tunisia. She was part of the organizing committee of the international conference Immersive Learning (ILRN) and of the scientific committee of the international scientific conference SLACTIONS (USA, Germany, Portugal, Spain, ...).
